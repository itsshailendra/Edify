Edify Privacy Policy


At Edify, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our e-learning platform for government job preparation.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:
  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact information.
  • Login Credentials: Username and password.
  • Course Data: Information about the courses you take, including progress and scores.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and device type.
  • Payment Information: Payment details, including credit/debit card information and billing address (if applicable).

    • How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to:
  • Provide and improve our services: To deliver courses, track progress, and provide support for government job preparation.
  • Communicate with you: To send notifications, updates, and promotional materials related to government job preparation.
  • Process payments: To securely process payments and manage subscriptions (if applicable).
  • Analyze and improve our platform: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform's performance.

    • How We Share Your Information


  • We may share your information with: Our affiliates and partners: To provide services, support, and promotional materials related to government job preparation.
  • Third-party service providers: To process payments, analyze data, and improve our platform.
  • Authorities: To comply with laws and regulations.

    • Data Security

    We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:
  • Encryption: We use encryption to protect sensitive data, such as payment information.
  • Secure servers: We store your data on secure servers that are regularly updated and monitored.
  • Access controls: We limit access to your data to authorized personnel only.


    • Your Rights

    You have the right to:
  • Access your data: To request a copy of your personal data.
  • Update your data: To update your personal data, including contact information.
  • Delete your data: To request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out of marketing: To opt-out of promotional materials and marketing communications.
